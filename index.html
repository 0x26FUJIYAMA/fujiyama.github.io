FAQ

Common questions.

Do my customers need to install anything?

No. They just message your existing LINE or WhatsApp account as usual. FUJIYAMA works behind the scenes.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

The bot stops responding until you subscribe. Your booking history in Google Sheets stays safe.

Can I use it for multiple businesses?

Standard covers 1 LINE channel. Pro covers up to 5. For larger operations, contact us.

What if the AI response is wrong?

Booking actions (create, cancel, check) run on our reliable rule engine — no AI involved. AI is only used for casual chat replies.

Is it available outside Thailand and the Philippines?

Yes. FUJIYAMA works anywhere LINE or WhatsApp is available. Currently optimised for SEA time zones.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, at any time via the PayPal subscription portal or by emailing us. No lock-in, no penalty.
